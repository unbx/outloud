About OutLoud

OutLoud turns any clip into a captioned audiogram that keeps the speaker's voice, or dubs it into another language in that same voice. Built on ElevenLabs, rendered in your browser.

  • Caption a clip. Live karaoke captions in the original language and voice.
  • Dub into 19 languages. Translated captions, still the original voice.

How your data is handled

No accounts, no logins, no analytics or tracking cookies. Here's where things go:

  • Your API key is stored only in your browser and sent straight to ElevenLabs with each request. It never reaches NANA's servers. Disconnecting clears it.
  • Clips and scripts you upload or write are sent to ElevenLabs to transcribe, narrate, or dub. Translations use Google Translate. NANA doesn't store any of it.
  • Your audiogram (captions, audio, and video) is rendered in your browser and downloaded by you. Nothing is uploaded to NANA.
  • Tester mode routes requests through a NANA proxy that adds the owner's key server-side. Your clip passes through on its way to ElevenLabs and isn't retained.

ElevenLabs and Google process data under their own policies. See ElevenLabs and Google.
